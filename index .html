What it does

A journal, not a checklist.

Most apps tell you what you heard, then forget it. BirdTRX keeps the whole moment — the bird, the place, the weather, the memory of the day.

Bird Song ID

Hear it, ID it, keep it.

Tap the microphone and BirdTRX identifies birds by song using BirdNET, right on your phone — no signal needed in the field. What you hear drops straight into your journal entry.

Rich diary entries

Every sighting becomes a memory.

Photos, voice notes and freely styled writing — each entry automatically stamped with the date, time, weather and location. A diary that's genuinely yours to keep.

Life list

Watch your life list grow.

Every sighting becomes a record — automatically. Tap any species to see every time and place you've encountered it, and export your records to eBird or BirdTrack.

Private by design

Your journal lives on your phone.

No account to create. No cloud lock-in. No advertising. BirdTRX runs bird song identification on your device — your recordings are never uploaded.

On-device ID

Bird song is identified on your phone, not on a server. It works with no signal.

No account needed

Start journaling straight away. Your data stays yours, always.

No ads, ever

A calm, quiet space to record your birding — nothing trying to sell to you.

Built on trusted science

Bird song identification is powered by BirdNET, from the K. Lisa Yang Center for Conservation Bioacoustics at the Cornell Lab of Ornithology. Birding sites and rarities are powered by eBird.
